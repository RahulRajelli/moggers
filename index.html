        01
        
    Every other tool scores you
    
        
    They tell you the match is 72% and sell you a rewrite. None of them check whether the parser can read the words at all. A score computed on text an ATS cannot extract is a number about nothing.
    
      
    
      
    
        02
        
    Ligatures are the silent killer
    
        
    Measured on real resumes: V&V, qualification and fixed-wing were all invisible to a parser until ligatures were disabled at the font level. The page looked identical either way.
